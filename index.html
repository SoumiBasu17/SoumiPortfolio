About Me

I'm an artist and creative professional dedicated to transforming ideas into visually captivating experiences. With a passion for sustainable art, innovative design, and meaningful creative expression, I bridge the worlds of art, education, and digital innovation.

My work spans from digital marketing and brand design to art therapy and workshop facilitation. I believe in the power of creativity to inspire change, foster inclusion, and create lasting impact. Every project is an opportunity to tell a story, evoke emotion, and make a difference.
